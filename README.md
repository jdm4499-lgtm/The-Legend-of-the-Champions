# The Legend of the Champions

A Breath of the Wild mod that brings the four champions to life as actual allies who fight alongside Link with their unique personalities, combat styles, and abilities.

## Features

### 🗡️ Champion Allies
- **Mipha** - Graceful healer with water-based support abilities
- **Daruk** - Strong defensive fighter with earth-based protection
- **Urbosa** - Confident leader with devastating electrical attacks
- **Revali** - Arrogant aerial combatant with precision wind attacks

### ⚔️ Personality System
Each champion retains their unique personality traits:
- **Dialogue/Voice Lines** - Context-aware comments during combat
- **Combat Behavior** - Unique fighting styles matching their character
- **Interactions** - Champion-to-champion dynamics and Link interactions
- **Special Moves** - Signature abilities reflecting their powers

### 🛡️ Dragon Armor Set
Unlock powerful dragon armor after completing all Divine Beasts:
- **Draganoid Chestplate** - Enhanced defense and elemental resistance
- **Mask of the Dragon** - Boosts special abilities and perception
- **Greaves of Lightning** - Increases speed and electrical affinity

### 📊 Progression System
Complete Divine Beasts to unlock champions and their abilities progressively.

## Champion Personalities

### Mipha
- **Trait**: Compassionate, supportive, gentle
- **Combat Role**: Healer & water elemental
- **Dialogue**: Encourages Link, offers healing wisdom
- **Special Ability**: Aqua Revival - Heals allies and temporary defense boost

### Daruk
- **Trait**: Strong, reliable, protective
- **Combat Role**: Tank & earth elemental
- **Dialogue**: Confident, straightforward compliments
- **Special Ability**: Boulder Breaker - Creates protective shield, knockback enemies

### Urbosa
- **Trait**: Confident, sassy, natural leader
- **Combat Role**: DPS & electricity specialist
- **Dialogue**: Witty comments, takes charge
- **Special Ability**: Fury - Lightning strikes multiple enemies, stuns foes

### Revali
- **Trait**: Arrogant, competitive, proud
- **Combat Role**: Aerial striker & wind specialist
- **Dialogue**: Boastful comments, competitive banter
- **Special Ability**: Gale - Launches into air, precision strikes, wind blasts
